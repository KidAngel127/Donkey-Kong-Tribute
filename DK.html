Donkey Kong Tribute Page

Welcome to my absolute very first project. I chose to make a web page about Super Smash Bros. Donkey Kong because i love games, I love meteo smashes and he has the sexiest meteor smashes in the game. For this project of mine i will talk about his kit and what I deem to be the best part about such ownage. Here you will learn about Donkey Kong's tips tricks and other cool mechanics.

GL HF
